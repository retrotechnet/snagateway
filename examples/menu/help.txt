HELP
----

Using this system:

  * At the LOGON screen, type your USERID and press ENTER, then type
    your PASSWORD and press ENTER.

  * At a MENU, type the number or letter shown beside an item and
    press ENTER to select it.

  * While reading a document, press ENTER to see the next page, or to
    return to the menu after the last page.

  * At any menu, type  B  to go BACK to the previous menu, or  X  to
    LOG OFF and return to the logon screen.

Sample logins for this demo:

    USERID: GUEST   PASSWORD: GUEST
    USERID: SYSOP   PASSWORD: SYSOP

Press ENTER to return to the menu.
