RELEASE NOTES
-------------

Gateway, menu edition:

  * Interactive USS menu system over the SSCP-LU session (no BIND).
  * Per-user login with starting menus.
  * Multi-level menus and submenus, with BACK and LOG OFF.
  * Paged text-file viewer.
  * Multiple concurrent terminals, one per LU.

Known limitations:

  * Display is monochrome line-mode (scrolling), not full-screen
    field mode - the SSCP-LU session is character-coded only.
  * Passwords are shown as typed (no field masking) and are stored
    in plaintext in the config; intended for an isolated network.

Press ENTER to return to the menu.
